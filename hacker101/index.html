Welcome to the Hacker101 Capture The Flag (CTF) Challenge Guide

This guide is designed to support your journey through the CTF challenges offered by Hacker101. These exercises are crafted to sharpen your skills in real-world web security concepts including vulnerability discovery, exploitation, and responsible disclosure.

We strongly encourage you to attempt each challenge using your own understanding and creativity first. The true value lies in the process, the more effort you invest, the deeper your knowledge will grow. Struggling is part of the learning curve, and every flag captured with your own reasoning brings you closer to becoming a skilled ethical hacker or bug bounty hunter.

Only refer to this guide if you're genuinely stuck and have tried multiple angles. Use it as a learning tool, not a shortcut

Thank you for your discipline and curiosity.
